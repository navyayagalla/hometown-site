Visit Ann Arbor

Enjoy diverse culture in this vibrant city home to the University of Michigan.

Top three activities to do in Ann Arbor

Visit Nichol's Arboretum

"The Arb", as it's known to Michigan students, is a great way to enjoy the wonderful flora and fauna of Ann Arbor.

Go Downtown

Downtown Ann Arbor boasts many fun activities including restaurants, movie theaters, and music venues.

Walk Around Campus

Get to know the University of Michigan by walking through the Diag or visiting the University of Michigan Museum of Art (UMMA).
